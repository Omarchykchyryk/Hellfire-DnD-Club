Come, wanderer...

Welcome to our modest mansions, wherther you are a level 14 elf, or a level 3 dwarf!
Now, there, of course, are some things to clarify. In case you're new here, you should know that, even though we call ourselves a "D&D club", it is far from the only thing we do. The genre is called "tabletop role-playing games", or TTRPG as we usually call it. Other than TRPG's, we also play traditional tabletop games, videogames, and watch stuff. Nerd club, if you will.
Despite what people think, we really don't bite. We often throw introductory games for the newbies for them to see if that whole thing is really for them.
There are superstitions about what we do. Check out the History of TTRPG if you'd like to know more about it and basically how out movement has developed.
